Securing Futures /
Improve Quality of Life

The Betty Adera Foundation is a nonprofit organization founded in 2019 by Betty Adera, born from a personal journey through adversity. Our mission is to empower vulnerable populations, enhance resilience, and promote equity through targeted, community-driven initiatives across Kenya, Tanzania, and Ghana.

We believe that every life matters, and that small, consistent changes create significant positive impacts over time. Through gender equality programs, education support, health initiatives, and economic empowerment, we strive to unlock potential and secure a better future for all.

Our Focus Areas

Gender Equality & GBV Prevention

Addressing sexual and gender-based violence through prevention programs, survivor support, and community education.

Education & Leadership Development

Empowering tomorrow's leaders through scholarships, mentorship programs, and leadership training initiatives.

Socio-Economic Empowerment & Smart Agriculture

Driving economic growth and sustainable farming practices that lift families out of poverty.

Climate Action & Environmental Sustainability

Protecting our planet through environmental conservation, green initiatives, and community climate education.

Health & Well-being

Improving access to healthcare, mental wellness resources, and holistic well-being services for all.

Research and Development

Developing innovation-driven, evidence-based solutions to address the root causes of social challenges.
