Mentorship Program

The Betty Adera Foundation Mentorship Program connects women and young leaders with experienced mentors who guide them toward personal growth, professional development, and community impact. Through a structured four-stage process, we create lasting, meaningful mentor-mentee relationships.

Mentorship Process

🤝

Rapport-Building

Rapport is simply the state of being relaxed and responsive with other people. Finding things you have in common is often the key to unlocking rapport in your relationship.

🔎

Direction-Setting

Having at least one clear goal or some tangible direction is important in creating a sense of purpose and urgency in a mentoring relationship. Without this focus, there will not be momentum.

📈

Progress-Making

Change is happening at ever-increasing rates in all domains. Working with us, you'll see new possibilities, achieve new levels of performance, and create authentic relationships — the foundation for breakthrough results.

✅

Winding Down

Every mentoring relationship has a natural conclusion. This phase celebrates the progress made, consolidates learnings, and transitions the mentee toward independence and continued growth.
