Who We Are

The Betty Adera Foundation is a nonprofit organization established in 2019 and headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya. We are dedicated to driving transformative and sustainable change in Kenya, Tanzania, and Ghana.

Founded by Betty Adera — a passionate social advocate for peace and justice — the foundation was born from a deeply personal journey. After sharing her own story about domestic violence, Betty found that her experience resonated with hundreds of women. From that spark of shared pain, a powerful movement was born.

Today, the Betty Adera Foundation stands as a beacon of hope for vulnerable communities, delivering programs that address gender-based violence, champion education, advance economic empowerment, and protect the environment.

We believe that every life matters, and that small, consistent changes create significant positive impacts over time.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To empower vulnerable populations, enhance resilience, and promote equity through targeted, community-driven programs that address the root causes of social inequality across Kenya, Tanzania, and Ghana.

Vision

A world where every individual — regardless of gender, background, or circumstance — has the opportunity to unlock their full potential and live a life of dignity, safety, and purpose.

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make and every program we deliver.

01

Do No Harm

We are committed to ensuring that our work does not inadvertently cause harm to the communities we serve or the individuals within them.

02

Embrace Diversity & Inclusion

We celebrate and respect all people regardless of gender, ethnicity, religion, or background, creating truly inclusive programs.

03

Safe Spaces for Dialogue

We create and protect safe environments where individuals can speak, share, and heal without fear of judgment or retaliation.

04

Professionalism

We uphold the highest standards of integrity, accountability, and professional conduct in all our operations and engagements.

05

Innovation

We continuously seek creative, evidence-based solutions to address complex social challenges in our communities.

06

Community-Centered

Every program is designed with and for the communities we serve, ensuring relevance, sustainability, and meaningful impact.

  • Safe spaces for dialogue and engagement
  • Professionalism
