Privacy Policy

Home › Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Betty Adera Foundation ("we", "our" or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your information when you visit our website (baf-website-tau.vercel.app). Please read it carefully. By using the site you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide to us

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Contact us via our Contact form (name, email address, message)
  • Apply to volunteer or for mentorship (name, email, phone, background details)
  • Send us an email or application directly

Automatically collected information

When you visit our website, certain information is collected automatically by your browser or our hosting provider, including your IP address, browser type, referring page and pages visited. This data is used in aggregate form to understand site usage and improve the user experience. We do not sell this data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries, applications and requests
  • Send you updates, newsletters and program information (only if you have opted in)
  • Improve our website content and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

We will never sell, rent or trade your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses browser localStorage only for functionality purposes — for example, to remember your preferences when reading blog posts. We do not use advertising cookies, Google Analytics tracking cookies, or any third-party ad networks. We do not display Google AdSense or similar advertising on this website.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website links to or embeds content from third-party services, including:

  • Google Fonts — to serve typefaces. Google may collect limited data per their Privacy Policy.
  • Google Drive / Dropbox — PDFs and reports may be hosted on these platforms. Their own privacy policies apply.
  • Formspree — our Contact, Volunteer, and Mentorship forms are processed by Formspree, which receives and stores the information you submit (name, email, phone number, and message) in order to deliver it to us. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • Social media platforms (Facebook, Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn) — links open external sites governed by their own policies.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external services.

5. Data Security

Our website is served exclusively over HTTPS (secure, encrypted connection). Contact, Volunteer, and Mentorship form submissions are processed and delivered to us through Formspree (see Section 4); we do not maintain our own database of form submissions. We implement reasonable measures to protect your information but cannot guarantee absolute security over the internet.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time by contacting us

8. Donation Information

Donations made through our website are processed via M-Pesa, PayPal or bank transfer as described on our Donate page. Payment processing is handled by the respective payment providers, who maintain their own security and privacy standards. We do not store payment card details on our website.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: info@bettyaderafoundation.org
  • Address: P.O. Box 4150-00100, Nairobi, Kenya
