Focus Areas

Home › Focus Areas

At Betty Adera Foundation, we believe every life matters. Our work is guided by six core focus areas that address the root barriers preventing vulnerable communities from achieving their full potential — across Kenya, Tanzania, and Ghana.

☠

Gender-Based Violence Prevention

We work at community level to prevent and respond to sexual and gender-based violence (SGBV). Through awareness campaigns, survivor support, and community engagement, we create safer environments for women, girls, and vulnerable populations.

🏫

Education & School Retention

We support adolescent girls and young mothers to remain in and return to school. By addressing financial, social, and cultural barriers, we ensure education remains accessible and that no girl is left behind because of circumstances beyond her control.

📈

Economic Empowerment

Financial independence transforms lives. We build the capacity of women and youth through skills training, entrepreneurship support, and access to economic opportunities — enabling them to become self-reliant contributors to their communities.

❤️

Sexual & Reproductive Health

Access to sexual and reproductive health rights (SRHR) is fundamental. We increase awareness and access to quality SRHR services, ensuring that women and girls have the information and support they need to make informed decisions about their health and bodies.

🌿

Climate Action

Climate change disproportionately affects the most vulnerable. We empower communities — particularly women and youth — to adopt sustainable practices, build climate resilience, and participate actively in environmental conservation and green livelihoods.

🔬

Research & Innovation

Sustainable solutions are evidence-based. Through research and innovation, we develop new approaches to complex social challenges, contributing to knowledge that strengthens our programmes and informs broader sector practice across East Africa.
