Empowering Young Mothers: The Role of Advocacy in School Re-Entry for Teen Moms in Kenya

As students across Kenya returned to school after a long holiday break, this was not the case for many young mothers who face significant barriers to re-entering the education system. BAF brought together advocates, educators and young mothers to discuss solutions and pathways forward.
