Be the Change You Want to See

Volunteering with the Betty Adera Foundation is an opportunity to contribute your time, skills, and passion to meaningful work that transforms lives. Whether you're local or international, we have a place for you.

👩‍🏫

Community Outreach

Join our teams in the field to conduct awareness campaigns and program delivery.

💻

Digital & Communications

Help us amplify our reach through content creation, social media, and digital storytelling.

⚖️

Legal & Advocacy

Legal professionals can support GBV survivors with pro bono legal guidance and rights advocacy.

💊

Health & Wellness

Healthcare professionals can join our mobile clinics and community health education initiatives.

📚

Education & Training

Educators can deliver workshops, skill-building sessions, and mentorship to youth.

🌿

Environmental Action

Join our climate action team for tree planting, cleanups, and environmental education.
