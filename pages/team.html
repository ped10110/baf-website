Management Team

Betty Adera

Chief Executive Officer & Founder

With 22 years of experience in senior management, Betty is a Public Health Specialist who has worked extensively with NGOs and international organizations. She previously led the DREAMS Program at Global Communities (USAID-funded) and has helped shape Sexual Reproductive Health Policies for young people across Kenya and globally. She holds an MSc in Public Health (University of Western Cape), a Postgraduate Diploma from the University of Erasmus, and a BA in Social Work (University of Nairobi), and is currently pursuing a PhD in Global Public Health. She is a UNESCO Books for Peace Award recipient (2019, 2020), CEO of the Year by Global Communities (2018), and holds a Humanitarian Medal of Honor from the World Peace and Diplomacy Organization (2019).

John Stanley Juma

Chief Financial Officer

Stanley oversees financial strategy, ensuring sustainability, transparency, and adherence to international financial standards. He brings extensive expertise in financial management, risk assessment, and strategic planning, leveraging tools like Power BI and SQL for data-driven decision-making. He holds an MBA from European Business University, an undergraduate degree from Daystar University, and is currently pursuing the Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) charter.

Petronila Owino

Marketing & Communications Specialist

Petronila is an accomplished sociologist with a strong reputation in youth and gender empowerment, bringing over a decade of NGO experience. She founded Enkare Oltau Foundation, focused on sustainable development and entrepreneurship for youth. She holds a double major in Sociology and Peace Studies from the University of Nairobi, an international volunteer management certification from Pista Magica (Portugal), and is a certified safe migration and human trafficking prevention facilitator through HAART Kenya. She has received international recognition from UN Volunteers for advancing sustainable volunteerism across Africa.

Nancy Yienya

Program Manager

Nancy provides strategic leadership for girls' and young women's empowerment programmes in Migori and Narok Counties. She leads the design, planning, budgeting, and implementation of integrated interventions addressing teenage pregnancy, early and forced marriage, FGM/C, GBV, and harmful social norms, in alignment with national AYSRH, child protection, and GBV frameworks. With over 15 years of experience, she has overseen high-impact, multi-sectoral programmes improving adolescent-responsive SRHR access, education retention, and economic empowerment. Her expertise spans program strategy, stakeholder engagement, and team leadership, grounded in her background in Nursing and Public Health.

Robert Otieno

Head of Human Resource Department

Robert is an experienced Administrator at the Betty Adera Foundation with over 10 years experience in HR Management. He is known for building team productivity and committed to aligning HR strategies with organizational goals. His leadership and professionalism have contributed to sustainable growth and staff success.
